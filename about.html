About Us

Over the past 120 years, cotton candy has become a favorite summer treat and a nostalgic candy for so many people! Cotton Candy has a special place in our hearts because it brings us back to simpler times. Our company strives to keep spinning cotton candy for you and your family to enjoy!

Events

Not only do we sell our cotton candy at your local retailer, we also do events! We will bring our machine and tent to your weddings, birthday party, or other event and serve your guests! This has always been a big hit with our customers.
